About the Exhibition

The Data Art 2026 Exhibition is part of the third-year Creative Visualisation course at Bangor University. Students explore innovative ways to represent data through visual and interactive media.

Led by Prof. Jonathan C. Roberts, the course fosters authentic learning experiences and public engagement through art and data.

The School of Computer Science and Engineering at Bangor University champions both visualisation research and education. We welcome new projects, partnerships, and collaborations across all forms of visual communication, from exploratory and explanatory graphics to data art and narrative-driven storytelling.

Let’s create something together—get in touch!

  • Data meets creativity: Authentic learning through data art design and exhibition
    Roberts, Jonathan C. (2026), Computers & Graphics
    DOI | Publisher

    Abstract: We introduce an authentic learning task where students create data art visualisations for public exhibition. Over multiple academic years, students engage in a structured process from research and design to reflection and curation. The framework supports creativity, technical skill development, and real-world application, demonstrating how data visualisation can connect academic learning with public engagement.
